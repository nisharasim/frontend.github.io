​WELCOME MESSAGE
Dear researchers/authors,
​
I welcome you to participate in the International Invention, Innovative & Creative (InIIC) Conference, 2020.  ​It is in conjunction with International Invention & Innovative Competition (InIIC), Series 2, 2020.

​STEP 5: PRESENTATION PREPARATION
Presentation via Google Meet will be held on 6 November 2020. A schedule of presentation and Google Meet link will be email a day before the conference. Please prepare a PPT file and get use to the Meet before the conference day (see guideline at the bottom).

To join the conference (presentation session):
  1. Click on the link or open link in a browser
  2. Turn off a microphone on the Meet to deactivate it (just click on the icon)
  3. Click Join
​You may participate in any session to hear other presentation and ask the questions in Q&A session by activate the microphone and camera. Make sure to turn it off when you've done to avoid noise disturbance to others.

Preparation few minutes before presentation:
Open PPT  and other files/web that will be used in the presentation

To start the presentation:
  1. Activate camera and microphone. 
  2. Click 'Present Now' > 'Your Entire Screen'
  3. Click the screen image > Click Share
  4. Start presentation by using a PPT or others
(Note: All viewers can see your computer/laptop screen)

To stop Presentation:
  1. Go to Meet > Click 'Stop Presenting'
  2. Turn off Microphone
​
​ (To practice with Meet before the conference day: Go to the website www.meet.google.com > Start a Meeting > Enter 'Test' as a code)
